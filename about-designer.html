About Freeternity

Co-founded by a self-taught designer Freeternity Rusinga, Freeternity Bead and Wire Furniture is a family owned business offering high quality and unique bead and wire furniture using the woven bead concept. The woven bead concept is achieved by using multiple strands of beaded wire which are twisted to form stiffened beaded wire ropes. The ropes can either consist of a single size of seed glass beads in three strands of beaded wire (1 x 3 woven bead rope) or two sizes of seed glass bead with each size having three strands of beaded wire (2x 3 woven bead rope). The woven bead concept is registered with IP Australia.
